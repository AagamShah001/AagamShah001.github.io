Welcome, Let’s Talk About Our Most Ask Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?
You can start from 40 pcs up to 2000 pcs, depending on size.
Do you provide design services?
Yes, we provide 3 proofs and 2 corrections. Design charges waived if printing is done with us.
How long does delivery take?
Normally 9–10 days across India (may vary slightly).
What are your payment terms?
30% advance is required, balance before dispatch.
Do you ship outside India?
Currently, we deliver only across India.
